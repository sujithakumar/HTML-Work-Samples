Steps
            On a computer, go to the YouTube video you want to embed.
            Under the video, click SHARE Share.
            Click Embed.
            From the box that appears, copy the HTML code.
            Paste the code into your HTML.

            Sample copied code 

            
                <iframe 

                width="560" height="315" 
                src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/9dMWsmaDYVo?si=3PCaKRPcuPXlnq5g"
                title="from YouTube" frameborder="0"

                allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; 
                picture-in-picture; web-share"

                allowfullscreen >

                </iframe>
